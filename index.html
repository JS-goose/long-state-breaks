What Is Breaking

Box breaking, or breaking as it's generally called, is a process of "breaking open" a box/case/pack of sports cards on a live stream. This is done so that others may enjoy the experience of seeing the cards opened.

Types of Breaks

  • Random Team Breaks
  • Pick Your Team
  • Team Draft Break
  • Hit Draft
  • Division Break
  • Auction Break
